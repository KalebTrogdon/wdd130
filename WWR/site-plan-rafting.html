Overview

Purpose

We are a team committed to providing the best experience in the west when it comes to white water rafting. We are here to provide the best experiences and enjoy the ride from Put-in to Take-out. We want to provide you with an enjoyable experience and with trusted guides. Here at Class VI Explorers, we pride ourselves on having the best guides that will keep you safe. While having us as guides or if you decide to go alone and only rent our boats, we hope you will have an enjoyable experience. Staff is always willing and able to help if you have any questions or problems. Whether those questions are water levels, weather, or problem points on the river they will provide up-to-date answers. Staff will also be posted at the worse places along the routes to provide you with conditions that may have changed and to provide you with extra safety. We also provide SOS beacons in each boat so emergency services can be called immediately if you require medical or search and rescue. At class VI Explorers we pride ourselves on handling any situation in a fast and professional manner. We hope you will provide us the opportunity to serve you while you are with us.

Audience

Are you tired of looking through rafting sites? tired of finding out the company does not provide what you need? Look to us because your long search is over. Whether you are a new rafter or an experienced rafter we have conditions for all. With our team of guides, we can navigate any situation and insure a fun and enjoyable experience.
